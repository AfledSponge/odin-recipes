Hot Pockets

Uhh I don't have many recipes, so I'll just tell you how to microwave a hot pocket properly. Y'know, in case you accidentally throw out the box and forget how long you're supposed to microwave them for.

Ingredients

  • Your preffered flavor of Hot Pocket
  • A microwave safe plate or maybe a paper towel

This recipe is really easy, lets get started.

  1. Open the box.
  2. Remove 1-2 Hot Pockets
  3. Dump the rest into the freezer and throw out the box. You won't need the insructions on the back, thats why I'm here
  4. Place your Hot Pocket(s) onto your plate or paper towel.
  5. Place your plate or paper towel into the microwave.
  6. If you are cooking one hot pocket, set the time for 1 minute and 30 seconds. For two, place it in for 3 minutes.
  7. Wait
  8. When it's done, wait some more.
  9. When you're done waiting, go ahead and claim your prize.
